Both sides — merge mode (left card and right card both have a deck)

In normal merge mode the left card is also a sublist head, so it gets its own peek deck splaying LEFT (--peek-dx: -1). The right deck splays RIGHT (--peek-dx: 1). Each deck's labels still sit bottom-centered on their peek cards.
